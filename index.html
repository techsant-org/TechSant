Got few questions in mine?

Well, let us try to read your mind and put some of those questions with answers below!

What exactly is TechSant?

TechSant is an open community who organizes free tech events among the ones who are interested in learning something new everyday and all the individuals out there who wants to share their knowledge with others.

What is different with TechSant?

Keeping in mind that along with learning and sharing, any individual would want to contribute to social cause too. So, we do support girls education from the contribution we get from kind souls.

What if I wanna join this NGO?

What if I wanna sponsor/contribute?

Yayy! So we found that kind soul. Just send a mail with little bit more about yourself and about how and what you wanna contribute.
